i'm trying to stay sane.

çağatay tanyıldız · february 14th, 2023

instead of social media updates, i keep a now page inspired by derek kedziora.

this is the 9th day of the disaster in my lovely country. i don’t how to feel about it anymore. it was not a surprise to us, as we had lots of similar disasters before. however, we were as unprepared as we were at the previous ones. nowadays, i have mixed feelings. my family is also affected very much not physically but mentally. i’m grateful that at least the harm was only mental. but there are still a lot of unknowns for my family on the horizon. however, i have nothing to do about it thanks to my family’s stubbornness and the patriarchal culture that craved in their bones. so to manage this burden in my inner self, i’m trying to focus on the books that i’m currently reading.

i’d like to share following quote from it which i can relate a lot. this is how i define my profession:

"This then is programming, both a tar pit in which many efforts have floundered and a creative activity with joys and woes all its own. For many, the joy far outweigh the woes, and for them the remainder of this book will attempt to lay some boardwalks across the tar."[1]

[1] The Mythical Man-Month, pg 9.
