this one would be a short update from me. from now on, i decided to keep the resources i like as bookmarks in my website rather than sharing it elsewhere. i also added those to my site's rss, so you should be able to track them easily.

the main motivations behind this change are;
- i don't want to trigger my lizard brain with likes, endorsements etc. that social media sites built on,
- i still want to share things that i think worth it, as long as those do not comprimise me.
